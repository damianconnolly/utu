Players Wanted in Upminster

Please see below for the teams we have currently looking for players to join our teams in Upminster and Hornchurch. Please get in contact with the team directly to arrange a training session. Although based in Upminster, we have many players from across Essex and Havering including Hornchurch, Cranham, Gidea Park, Romford, Brentwood, Rainham, Collier Row and Harold Wood.

If you would like to consider bringing your team to Upminster Town United, please Contact Us

U11 Raptors

U11 Raptors (U12 from September 2026) are looking for attacking and defensive players to join our mid-level BCFA division squad. Please contact Damian on 078282 12334 to arrange a trial. Training is twice a week, on Saturday mornings and Wednesday evenings.

U14 Blues

U14 Blues (U15 from September 2026) are looking for midfield and defensive players to join our BCFA Div 3 division squad. Please contact Andrew on 07904 237627 to arrange a trial. Training is on Thursday evenings on astroturf.

U16 Stripes

U16 Stripes are looking for an attacking player to join our BCFA Championship division squad. Please contact Pete on 07956 379817 to arrange a trial. Training is on Thursday evenings on a 4G pitch.
