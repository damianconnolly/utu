Upminster Town United Youth Football Club

We are a grassroots football club based in Upminster and Hornchurch, established in 2017 and playing in the BCFA and Echo football leagues.

For season 2025/26, we have nine teams representing the club, with over 120 boys and girls playing matches across four great locations in Upminster and Hornchurch, Havering. If you are interested in joining the club, please review our Players Wanted page.

As a youth football club, we are happy to welcome new children to our training sessions in Upminster, and to learn and develop their football skills while having fun.
