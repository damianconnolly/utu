Upminster Town United Youth Football Club

We are a grassroots football club based in Upminster and Hornchurch, established in 2017 and playing in the BCFA and Echo football leagues.

For season 2026/27, we have ten teams representing the club, with over 120 boys and girls playing matches across four great locations in Upminster and Hornchurch, Havering. If you are interested in joining the club, please review our Players Wanted page.

As a youth football club, we are happy to welcome new children to our training sessions in Upminster, and to learn and develop their football skills while having fun.

From Autumn 2026, we plan to start out Keepie Uppies section - a fun Saturday morning session for younger children to pick up some football skills and make great friends, in a safe environment. We are currently taking pre-regstrations now, so please register your interest.
