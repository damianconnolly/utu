Upminster Town United are a grassroots football club, formed in 2017.




Starting with a single team, the club has continued to grow and have had teams playing in every age group from U7 to U17 over the last 8 years.

Our teams are proud members of the Brentwood Community Football Association and the Echo Junior Football League, and are an FA Accredited club under Essex FA. The majority of our teams have formed as non-competitive kids' sections from the age of 6, which have evolved into teams taking part in regular matches through organised leagues, tournaments and cup competitions throughout Havering and Essex.

For 2025/26 we have 9 teams representing the club - over 140 boys and girls from Upminster and the surrouncing area (including Thurrock, Hornchurch, Romford, Harold Wood, Harold Hill, Gidea Park, Brentwood and Rainham) playing and training at multiple private venues across Upminster, Hornchurch, Romford and Harold Hill.

We are proud to be a small close knit club, promoting values including sportsmanship, development and enjoyment of the game at all levels. If you are interested in joining the club, please see our Players Wanted section or contact us if you'd like to discussing bringing a team under our umbrella to represent our Claret and Blue kit with very low fees.
